Our Story & Leadership

The Heartland of Dumarao, Capiz

Our Heritage

Formerly known as Barrio Site, our community served as the vital central hub for surrounding Sitios. History tells us that the name “Bungsuan” was born from the collision of nature.

Derived from the Hiligaynon words “Bunggo” or “Bungsu”, it describes the point where the powerful Badbaran River crashes against the massive rock formations that define our borders.

Today, we stand proud as an urbanized upland community, celebrated as the “Maisan sang Banwa” for our golden corn fields that sustain our town.
