About Our Community

Barangay Bungsuan is one of the peaceful and growing communities in the Municipality of Dumarao. Known for its warm and welcoming residents, the barangay reflects the true spirit of Bayanihan.

From our lush agricultural lands that celebrate our identity as a corn-producing hub to our active local council, we strive to provide a safe and prosperous environment for all Bungsuanons. We take pride in our heritage and our collective vision for a brighter future.
